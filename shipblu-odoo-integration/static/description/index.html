ShipBlu Connector for Odoo

Seamlessly connect your Odoo 18 system to your ShipBlu shipping account.

Features

  • Create shipping labels automatically from your sales orders.
  • Track shipment statuses in real time inside Odoo.
  • Get instant shipping prices and compare rates.
  • Receive critical updates about delivery progress and exceptions.

Benefits

  • Save time by avoiding manual label creation.
  • Provide better visibility and service to your customers.
  • Reduce errors and improve operational efficiency.

How It Works

  1. Install this module in your Odoo 18 instance.
  2. Configure your ShipBlu API credentials in the settings.
  3. Start creating labels and tracking shipments directly from Odoo.
